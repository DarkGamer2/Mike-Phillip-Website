HISTORY

Mike Phillip Limited is a limited liability company established in 1978, specializing in surface preparation and painting works. We have over thirty years experience serving building owners,architects and specifiers. Management has over thirty six (36) years experience and our capabilities go beyond ladders, trucks and brushes to the preparation and coating field, ranging from small decorative works to major sandblasting.

Our mission is to serve our clients in a most effective, timely, coordinated and cost effective manner which would domino into Mike Phillip Limited becoming the preferred choice for painting contractor services. We are also committed to serving all our clients (residential, commercial, industrial) with professionalism and the notion that “the clients come first.”
