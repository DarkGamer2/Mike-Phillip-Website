SERVICES OFFERED

INDUSTRIAL SERVICE

The scope of industrial site painting, preparation and coating which MPL carries out, ranges from warehouses to factory installations. It also includes aggressive surface preparation techniques and specialized coatings.

After more than thirty years we’ve seen almost everything, very little would surprise us. Whether under a shutdown or fully active, industrial work makes safety and liability protection even more challenging since hazards of almost any description can exist.

The people at Mike Phillip Limited are experts in industrial painting. Several operational highlights contribute to our qualifications and meet or exceed protocols.

COMMERICAL SERVICE

The commercial painting category covers a broad range of environments and applications including interior and exterior work in office buildings, apartments/condominium complexes and more.

Mike Phillip Limited is substantially broad in scope and ability. Our operational systems ensure that you get the right manpower on your job when you need them; day, night, week-ends, holidays, even tomorrow morning when an emergency arises.
